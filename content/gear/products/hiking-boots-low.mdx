The Salomon X Ultra 4 is the gold-standard low-cut hiking boot. Used by professionals and praised by amateurs alike, this boot combines weight savings with genuine protection and performance.

The Gore-Tex waterproofing is genuinely effective—your feet stay dry even in serious weather. The Contagrip outsole offers exceptional grip on wet, muddy terrain (critical in the Benelux where rain is constant). The heel cushioning is outstanding, protecting your knees and joints during long descents.

At just 350g per shoe, these are remarkably light. Many hikers use them for trail running as well as serious hiking. The fit is true to size with a slightly roomier toe box—important for preventing hot spots and blisters.

Perfect for day hikes, standard trails, and active hikers prioritizing speed and comfort over maximum protection. Not ideal for extremely technical terrain or heavy backpacking.
