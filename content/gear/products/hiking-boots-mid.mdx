The Scarpa Zodiac Plus GTX is the serious hiker's choice. The mid-cut design provides crucial ankle support on steep, rocky terrain—particularly important for Ardennes and Zuid-Limburg routes with significant elevation and uneven ground.

The Vibram sole is legendary for durability and grip. These boots will easily last 1,500+ km of consistent hiking if maintained properly. The Gore-Tex waterproofing works brilliantly. The overall build quality is exceptional—Scarpa is an Italian company with over 70 years of mountaineering heritage.

The ankle collar is padded and designed for comfort despite the mid-cut height. Many hikers carry these for demanding routes and switch to lighter shoes for easy days. The precision fit takes a break-in period (50-100km) but the payoff is outstanding comfort on technical terrain.

Ideal for mountain hikes, hilly terrain, multi-day treks, and serious elevation gain. Less suitable for flat walks or speed-focused day hikes.
