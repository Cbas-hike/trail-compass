The Keen Targhee III Waterproof offers exceptional value for hikers on a budget. The wide toe box is genuinely spacious—perfect for hikers with wider feet or those who prefer roomy boots.

The waterproofing is effective, and the thick rubber toe cap provides protection on rocky terrain. These are popular with beginners and casual day hikers. The sole provides adequate grip, though not as advanced as premium options.

The design is utilitarian rather than sophisticated, but the durability is solid and the comfort is surprisingly good. Many hikers love the value proposition: decent performance at a significantly lower price point than premium competitors.

Ideal for beginners, casual hikers, wet terrain, and those prioritizing value. The wide toe box makes these excellent for hikers with specific fit requirements.
