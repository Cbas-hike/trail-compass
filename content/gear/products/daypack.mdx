The Osprey Talon 22 is the most popular day hiking backpack for good reason. The 22L size is perfectly calibrated for full-day hikes—room for layers, snacks, hydration, and safety gear without excessive bulk.

The ventilated back panel is genuinely comfortable even after hours of hiking. The hipbelt distributes weight effectively, protecting your shoulders from fatigue. Side pockets hold water bottles, and top pockets keep essentials accessible.

At just 950g (2.1 lbs), this is remarkably light for its capacity. Osprey's lifetime warranty means you're protected against defects forever. The pack is genuinely durable—these commonly last 5+ years of regular use.

The design is functional rather than aesthetic, but that's appropriate for an outdoor-focused tool. Compatible with hydration bladders if you prefer hands-free drinking on long routes.
